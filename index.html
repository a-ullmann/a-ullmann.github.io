About Me

My first interests for software engineering began at a young age. I had the unique opportunity to help build a music-blog with two friends, giving me the chance to learn the basics of how websites are structured and run. This laid the foundation for my journey into software engineering, fuelling my ambition to learn more. Following my studies in International Management, I obtained a position as a workforce coordinator in the customer service department of a cryptocurrency exchange. This experience gave me an understanding of blockchain technology and an insight into how an online business functions.

This motivated me to pursue a career in software engineering, allowing me to take my knowledge and understanding to the next level. I am ambitious to learn more and take my career to the next level, and I am excited to bring my enthusiasm, creativity and ambition to the software engineering world.
